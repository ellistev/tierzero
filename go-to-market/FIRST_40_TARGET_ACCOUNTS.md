# TierZero First 40 Target Accounts

## Purpose
This is the first source-backed target slate for the **Overnight Service Desk Queue Clearance** wedge.

It is not a random logo wall.
It is a ranked working list of accounts that show some combination of:
- strong overnight or multi-shift operations
- regulated or audit-sensitive environments
- employee support at meaningful scale
- direct or indirect evidence of ServiceNow or mature ITSM usage
- obvious expansion paths beyond password reset / account unlock

## How to read this list
### Priority A
Best first targets.
Either direct ServiceNow evidence exists, or the overnight queue pain is so plausible that qualification should start here.

### Priority B
Good targets.
Strong fit signals are present, but either the ServiceNow proof or the overnight pain proof is slightly weaker.

### Priority C
Worth keeping in the slate, but needs deeper qualification before outreach time gets expensive.

## Buyer roles to target across the slate
Default titles:
- Head of Service Desk
- Director of End User Support
- IT Support Manager
- Director of IT Operations
- CIO or VP of IT
- ServiceNow Platform Owner
- IAM / Identity Lead
