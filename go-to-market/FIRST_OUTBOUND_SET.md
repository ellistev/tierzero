## Discovery opener

I’m not trying to sell you broad AI transformation.

I just want to find one repetitive ticket type your team already hates, prove we can remove it from the queue safely, and measure the before/after.

If we cannot point to a clear backlog or SLA improvement, it is not a fit.

---

## Qualifying questions
1. What ticket class is repetitive enough that your team hates touching it?
2. How much of the queue is runbook-backed today?
3. What happens to the queue overnight?
4. What would make this obviously worth paying for after 30-45 days?
5. What approval / audit constraints would kill the deal if we cannot satisfy them?

---

## CTA options
- Worth a 15-minute look?
- Want the pilot outline?

---

## Positioning guardrails
- Sell queue reduction, not AI magic
- Sell one workflow first, not platform breadth
- Sell auditability and safety, not autonomy theater
- Sell labor displacement carefully - focus on backlog, SLA, and exception handling
